FACILITES

CANTEEN / SHOP


The canteen here is owned by Haldiram's and is open 24-hours and provides lots of tasty but nutritious food items at very affordable prices for the young champions. The sports goods are owned by Nike and provide the best quality equipments for all sports.

SECURITY / PARKING


The whole campus of the academy (including parking) is guarded by black cat commandos and CRPF and is purely safe for young stars. The parking here can accomodate every kind of vehicle and the vehicles are guarded by security.

RESTROOMS / SHOWERS


The restrooms here are purely neat and clean and are equiped with every facilities of modern days and the showers provide the qualities of facilities for the children to take shower after swimming.
