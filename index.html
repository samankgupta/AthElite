Welcome To AthElite Sports Complex

AthElite acknowledges that sports today play a vital role in all-round development of a human being and achieving excellence in sports has a great bearing on national prestige and morale.

AthElite Sports Academy is one of the most proclaimed academies of India. The academy has not only employed experienced coaches who have represented their country in one or the other sport, but also has the privilage of producing many of the famous sporting legends who are currently representing us on national level. Virendra Sehwag, Gautam Gambhir (cricket), Sunil Chhetri (football), Sania Mirza (lawn tennis) are to name a few sportspersons produced by this academy.


The academy has the finest coaches,world class tracks,courts for every sports. The academy also has facilities for parking and also has a shop for sports goods. There are world-class restrooms and showers a canteen with lots of nutritiuous food items for the growing players. There is also a 24-hour security in the academy.


The institution also provides its customers with a membership facility (monthly, yearly or lifetime) so that they can easily pay once and take the pleasure of every sport and every facility.
