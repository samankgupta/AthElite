MEMBERSHIP

Monthly Membership


Monthly membership is very useful. If you will take monthly membership then you can leave it any time if you want to. In this u will get some facilities like you can do swimming any time free of cost. The fees for monthly membership is Rs. 2500 per month.

Yearly Membership


Yearly membership is again a beneficial membership. In this you pay every year no problem of month charges. In this also you can do swimming any time & have 520 hrs extra. Fees for yearly membership is Rs.11000/annum only.

Lifetime Membership


Lifetime membership is very beneficial. Moneywise and facilities as well. If you are interested in lifetime membership then carry your id proof. Fees for lifetime membership is for just Rs. 60,000 once.
